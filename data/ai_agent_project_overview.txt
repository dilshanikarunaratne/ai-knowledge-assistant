AI Agent Project Overview

Project Name:
Smart Business Automation Assistant

Purpose:
The Smart Business Automation Assistant helps small businesses reduce repetitive manual work by using artificial intelligence, document search, and workflow automation.

Main Features:
The system can answer questions from company documents, search policy files, summarize uploaded documents, and help employees find information faster.

Target Users:
The main users are small business owners, administrative staff, customer support teams, clinics, law firms, schools, ecommerce shops, and consulting companies.

Technology Stack:
The system uses Python, Streamlit, Ollama, FAISS, sentence-transformers, and retrieval-augmented generation.

RAG Workflow:
Documents are uploaded into the system. The system splits documents into chunks, creates embeddings, stores them in a vector database, retrieves relevant chunks, and sends them to a local language model to generate answers.

Reliability:
The system includes a QA validation step that checks whether the generated answer is supported by the retrieved context.

Future Improvements:
Future versions may include LangChain, LangGraph, multi-agent workflows, email automation, calendar automation, and robotic process automation features.

Business Value:
The system saves time, reduces manual searching, improves employee productivity, and helps businesses make better use of internal knowledge.
